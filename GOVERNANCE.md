# Governance

## Maintainers

tinybpf is maintained by [Kyle Seneker](https://github.com/kyleseneker).

## Decision making

Decisions about the project — features, releases, breaking changes — are made by the maintainer. Community input via issues and discussions is welcome and valued.

## Becoming a maintainer

Consistent, high-quality contributions over time may lead to maintainer access. There is no formal process — it's based on trust and demonstrated judgment.

## Reporting issues

- **Bugs**: Open an issue with a minimal reproducer
- **Security**: See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for responsible disclosure
- **Features**: Open an issue describing the problem, your proposed solution, and alternatives considered
